Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a very popular dish across East and Southeast Asia.

This recipe is my own take on the dish, based on the availability of ingredients in my place.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Green onion
  • Onion
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cooked rice
  • Egg
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. As a previous step, grab a cup of rice and cook it the day prior.
  2. Chop some carrots, Zucchinis, green onion, and onion.
  3. Heat up a wok (or a pan if you don't have a wok) and add some sesame oil.
  4. When the oil gets hotter, add carrots first, cook for a minute or two and then add zucchini, green onions, and onions. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Afterwards, add the cooked rice and cook for 2 to 5 minutes.
  6. Then, grab an egg, break it and scramble it. Make space in the center of the pan and add a little bit of oil, put the egg in there and cook.
  7. After a minute or two, add your desired amount of soy sauce to the rice. After a few minutes, turn off the heat and let it cool for a little, then serve.
