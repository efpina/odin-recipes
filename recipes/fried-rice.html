Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a very popular dish across East and Southeast Asia.

This recipe is my own take on the dish, based on the availability of ingredients in my place.

Ingredients

  • 200g - Cooked rice
  • 60ml - Sesame oil
  • 1/2 - Onion
  • 1 - Zucchini
  • 1 - Carrot
  • 65ml (to taste) - Soy sauce
  • 2 - Eggs
  • 5 - Green onions

Steps:

  1. As a previous step, grab a cup of rice and cook it the day prior.
  2. Cut the carrot, zucchini, green onions, and onion. It should result in very small pieces.
  3. Heat up a wok (or a pan if you don't have a wok) and add sesame oil.
  4. When the oil gets hotter, add the carrot first, cook for a minute or two and then add zucchini, green onions, and onions. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Afterwards, add the cooked rice and cook for 2 to 5 minutes.
  6. Then, grab the eggs, break them and scramble them. Make space in the center of the wol or pan, add a little bit of sesame oil, put the eggs there and cook.
  7. After a minute or two, add your desired amount of soy sauce to the rice. After a few minutes, turn off the heat and let it cool for a little, then serve.
