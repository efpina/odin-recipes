Entomatadas

Description

Easy Mexican recipe

This Entomatadas recipe serves as a gentle introduction to Mexican cooking

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Serrano peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cheese
  • Corn tortillas
  • Sour cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Roast the tomatoes and serrano peppers. It's better if you have a comal around, but if you don't have one you can grab some aluminum foil and roast them with a pan, or even directly into the fire.
  2. At the same time, chop onions into thumb-sized pieces, finely chop garlic and pan fry them with oil until the garlic turns golden brown.
  3. Put the roasted tomatoes and peppers, and the onion and garlic in a blender alongside oregano and start blending until liquified.
  4. Heat up a pan (can be the same onions and garlic pan or could be a different one) and add some oil, after gettinng hotter put the recently blended sauce into it.
  5. Cook the sauce with low heat for 10 to 15 minutes, stir occasionally.
  6. In another pan, add the oil so that we fry tortillas for the dish.
  7. Fry the desired amount of tortillas in the pan and dry them afterwards.
  8. When all the tortillas are ready, dip them into the sauce, add chopped onions and cheese and roll them over. Repeat for all tortillas.
  9. After that, add some more sauce, onions and (optionally) sour cream to complete the dish.
