Entomatadas

Description

Easy Mexican recipe

This Entomatadas recipe serves as a gentle introduction to Mexican cooking

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • 650g - Tomatoes
  • 2 - Serrano peppers (optional)
  • 1/2 - Onion
  • 3 - Garlic cloves
  • Oregano
  • 1/2 Cup of water
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon - Salt
  • Vegetable oil

You'll also need:

  • 20 - Corn tortillas
  • 1/2 - Onion
  • 200g - Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup - Sour cream
  • Vegetable oil

You might also need aluminun foil to help roast the tomatoes and peppers.

Steps:

  1. Roast the tomatoes and serrano peppers. It's better if you have a comal around, but if you don't have one you can grab some aluminum foil and roast them with a pan, or even directly into the fire. Set them apart when they're roasted and have a soft consistency.
  2. At the same time, chop onions into thumb-sized pieces and sauté them alongside the garlic cloves with oil until the onion parts change color. Save the pan for a later step.
  3. Gather the roasted tomatoes and peppers, and the onion and garlic in a blender alongside oregano and half a cup of water, and start blending until the mix liquifies.
  4. In the same pan you sautéed the onions and garlic in, add vegetable oil and put the recently blended sauce into it. Add some salt for flavor.
  5. Cook the sauce with low heat for 10 to 15 minutes until it gets a thicker consistency, stir occasionally.
  6. In another pan, add some oil so that we can fry tortillas for the dish.
  7. Fry the desired amount of tortillas (20) in the pan and dry them afterwards.
  8. When all tortillas are ready, dip them into the sauce, add chopped onions and cheese and roll them over. Repeat for all tortillas.
  9. After that, add some more sauce, onions and sour cream to complete the dish.
